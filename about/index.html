Legality & Transparency Statement

Security by transparency. FreshVPN heavily relies on completely legitimate and public utilities. All parsing routines, compilation sequences, and script components utilized to aggregate the configurations derive from open-source GitHub ecosystems available for public inspection.

No proprietary bypasses are built behind closed doors. The infrastructure leverages existing networking protocols to maintain continuous uptime without violation of digital agreements.
